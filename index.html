Welcome to my super official website. Today's subject: debate!

What is debate? Debate can mean many things! It ranges from the most basic of human arguments (eg: your weekly interaction with that teacher), to whatever those old folk are talking about in their fancy G7 meetings.

For us, debating is a sport of the mind. We start by learning the basics of building arguments, then slowly build up to working in a team, so that we can then go to contests and olympiads with our newfound skills. Then brag about it for the rest of eternity.
