2. In Romania

In terms of academical debate, it was mostly brought here through various NGOs. Evidently, it didn't really exist formally before 1989. A few years after that, ARDOR (Asociatia Romana de Dezbateri, Oratorie si Retorica, en: Romanian Association for Debate, Rhetoric and Oratory) was founded, initially as a network of several highschool clubs nationwide, then united into one big association, now divided in six regions for administration purposes.

Today, countless highschool students are able do develop their soft skills and debating/argumentative skills in these ARDOR-affiliated clubs. There are also MUN competitions, handled by a different NGO.
